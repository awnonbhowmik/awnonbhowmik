# Hello, I am Awnon Bhowmik

## Researcher | Software Engineer | Educator

<div align="justify">

I am a researcher and software engineer working at the
intersection of cybersecurity, data analytics, software
development, and reproducible research. I am currently
pursuing a Doctor of Computer Science in Cybersecurity and
Information Assurance at Colorado Technical University, and
I completed my Master's degree in Data Analytics and
Cybersecurity from Colorado State University Global with a
4.0 GPA.

My academic background also includes associate's and
bachelor's degrees in Mathematics from CUNY institutions,
along with a minor in Computer Science. Over time, my work
has expanded from mathematics and problem solving into
software engineering, information security, cryptography,
data-driven analysis, and privacy-aware systems.

In addition to my engineering and academic work, I have
more than a decade of experience as an educator, having
taught mathematics across multiple CUNY colleges and served
as a substitute teacher for the New York City Department of
Education. This background continues to shape how I
approach clarity, structure, and communication in both
software and research.

</div>

## Research and Reproducibility

<div align="justify">

My work focuses on software engineering, data analytics,
cybersecurity, and reproducible research. I am especially
interested in how data-driven methods can strengthen cyber
defense, improve threat detection, and support
privacy-aware systems.

My research interests include information security,
machine learning for predictive threat analysis,
differential privacy, cryptography, and practical systems
that connect technical rigor with real-world impact. I am
also interested in interdisciplinary, data-driven research
that extends beyond cybersecurity into broader scientific
and public-interest domains.

</div>
